# Fresh Beats Project Status Report

## Project Description
This project analyzes user engagement and growth trends for Fresh Beats, a music streaming platform. The report compares free and paid users, reviews listening activity over time, and identifies opportunities to improve user conversion, retention, and artist promotion. The goal was to summarize business insights and provide recommendations that could help Fresh Beats increase paid subscriptions and improve platform performance.

## Business Problem
Fresh Beats needs to better understand how free and paid users engage with the platform. Free users may have lower engagement and retention, while paid users show stronger listening activity and steadier growth. This project helps identify ways to convert more free users into paid subscribers and improve long-term user engagement.

## Key Insights
- Paid users are more active than free users and show steadier growth over time.
- Paid users contribute more engagement value to the platform.
- Rock is the strongest genre among free users.
- Electronic, hip hop, and indie show upward momentum, especially among paid users.
- Fresh Beats has opportunities to improve conversion from free to paid subscriptions through targeted promotions and pricing strategies.

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize strategies that convert free users into paid subscribers.
- Offer limited-time free trials, referral incentives, or introductory pricing.
- Use genre-focused promotions based on user listening preferences.
- Improve artist promotion by focusing on genres with strong growth momentum.
- Continue monitoring user retention and engagement trends by subscription type.
